In regard to the recent events surrounding ClearSky

March 21, 2024

While I was away from BlueSky for the last week there have been incidents involving a co-developer of ClearSky. It has been decided that I no longer will be working with my cohort, effective immediately. Thank you to those who came to me rather than allowing my name to continue to be misrepresented, and to those who continue to show their faith in my work as I pour my energy into building and improving ClearSky.

Justin, Creator of ClearSky
